Wesbite Description

My name is Abhinav Tadikonda and I'm a junior studying Information Analysis at the University of Michigan. As part of my SI 339 class I am developing a website using skills I have aquired thoughout the semester.

Art history is the study of the history and development of painting, sculpture, and the other visual arts. This website includes galleries of both contemporary and historical art in order to provide visual examples of the differences between the two types of art.

Click on any of the images on the "Contemporary Artwork" or "Historical Artwork" pages to be redirected to more information about the peice of art!
